We received a narrow-band ping from a lone explorer running damaged comms hardware several thousand light years beyond ordinary traffic. The signal was thin, but deliberate.

Science and Flight Operations pieced together enough telemetry to forward a route correction and an updated carrier schedule. We could not offer immediate extraction, but we could give them something useful: a reachable destination and proof that someone heard them.

There are moments that remind a crew why this vessel exists. Tonight was one of them.
