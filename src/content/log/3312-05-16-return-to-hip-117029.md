We arrived in HIP 117029 today, at the end of a return journey that began on the far side of Colonia. The system should have felt familiar before it felt anything else.

Instead, our first reports confirmed that control has passed to the HIP 117029 pirates. Stella Nebula Project no longer holds its own origin system. After a year spent measuring distant arms of the galaxy, it is a strange thing to come home and find the nearest work waiting unresolved at the doorstep.

The Moonshield will remain available in support of Stella Nebula Project operations while efforts begin to restore lawful control of the system. We crossed a great distance to return here. We do not intend to stand aside now that we have.
