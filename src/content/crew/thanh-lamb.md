Thanh Lamb keeps the commodity market running smoothly even when half the station deck thinks its cargo should move first. She balances contract demand, available storage and current carrier priorities with impressive restraint.

Before joining the carrier, Lamb built her reputation brokering small but reliable margins on difficult routes. She still believes consistency is worth more than spectacle, especially when fuel and timing are involved.

Pilots trust her numbers because she rarely promises anything the market cannot actually support.
