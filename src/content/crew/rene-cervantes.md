René Cervantes runs refuel operations like a heartbeat of the carrier: constant, reliable and never dramatic unless something has already gone wrong. He understands better than most that fuel availability changes how far a pilot is willing to go.

He came up through support crews attached to rescue and logistics wings, where speed mattered but mistakes mattered more. That balance still defines his approach.

On busy rotations, he knows exactly which ships need to move first and which commanders should probably stop improvising.
