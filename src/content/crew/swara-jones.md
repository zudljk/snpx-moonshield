Swara Jones is the head of catering on the fleet carrier Moonshield. Born and raised on Cemiess, the homeworld of Imperial Princess Aisling Duval, she spent her youth in a sheltered environment shaped by traditional values and high expectations. However, against her parents’ wishes, Swara decided to pursue a career in the restaurant business. With passion and entrepreneurial spirit, she first built a successful bar and eventually opened a hotel that soon became a popular gathering place.

Although her business was thriving, Swara had always dreamed of traveling among the stars and experiencing life in space. When she was offered the chance to take over management of the onboard bar and dining operations on the privately operated fleet carrier Moonshield, she didn’t hesitate for long. She sold her hotel and began a new chapter in her life, combining her passion for the culinary arts with the adventure of interstellar travel.

Although Swara isn’t directly involved in running the bar on the Moonshield, she spends a lot of time there, soaking up the stories of the space pilots and travelers. Her presence and her interest in her guests’ adventures have made the onboard bar a popular spot for socializing and storytelling, giving the Moonshield a particularly welcoming atmosphere. Swara Jones is known as a warm host who combines the art of hospitality with the vastness of space.
