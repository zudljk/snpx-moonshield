Otis Nelson took command of *FC Wayfarer's Rest* after years escorting long-haul convoys through unstable trade corridors. He is known for clear orders, measured judgment and the rare ability to make a crowded deck feel orderly.

Most of the crew describe him as unhurried rather than lenient. He listens closely, expects precision and has little patience for preventable chaos during jump prep.

Passengers usually remember him for the calm voice on the public channel just before departure. On a carrier like this, that kind of calm matters.
