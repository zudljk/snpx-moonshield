Echo Park is responsible for the Tritium Depot, which on a fleet carrier means protecting the one resource nobody forgets once it starts running low. Park monitors reserve levels, transfer timing and consumption forecasts with almost unnerving accuracy.

They built that reputation on deep-range support assignments where every jump had to justify itself. Waste was not an inconvenience there; it was a route-ending mistake.

Since joining *Wayfarer's Rest*, Park has become the person command consults whenever plans grow ambitious and fuel margins grow thin.
