Alejandra McLeod is the carrier's point of contact for Universal Cartographics, and few people aboard are happier to see a dusty explorer than she is. She knows that behind every data upload there is usually a long route, a tired pilot and at least one story worth hearing.

Her work is equal parts analysis, verification and diplomacy. Explorers want quick turnaround, command wants clean records and both sides expect her desk to make that happen.

McLeod somehow manages it while still sounding genuinely interested in what crews found out there.
