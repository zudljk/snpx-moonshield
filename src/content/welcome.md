o7 CMDRs! Welcome aboard the [SNPX] Moonshield.

My name is CMDR Ronin-667, owner and CEO of this Fleet Carrier. Whether you have arrived here as a trader, explorer, mercenary, passenger, technician, or simply as a traveller in need of a safe place to dock, I am pleased to have you with us.

The Moonshield was built to be more than a mobile platform of steel, fuel, and flight decks. She is a waypoint, a shelter, and a reliable base of operations far from the comfort of settled space. Out here, every commander knows the value of a safe harbor, a stocked market, a capable crew, and a moment of quiet before the next jump.

While you are aboard, I ask only that you respect the ship, the crew, and your fellow guests. Our staff are here to keep the carrier running, to support your operations, and to make your stay as comfortable as possible. In return, we expect professionalism, patience, and a reasonable appreciation for the fact that several thousand tonnes of machinery are working very hard to keep everyone alive.

Please make use of our services, enjoy the facilities, and keep an eye on the carrier announcements for departure schedules, service updates, and operational notices. If the Moonshield is about to move, you will be informed in good time — though I strongly recommend not leaving your ship parked in the hangar with the engines cold and your navigation plans undecided.

On behalf of myself and the entire crew: welcome to the Moonshield. May your cargo be profitable, your scans be valuable, your ammunition sufficient, and your return journey safe.

CMDR Ronin-667
